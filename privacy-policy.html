Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 14, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 14, 2026

BizBot SA ("we", "our", or "us") provides a WhatsApp-based business assistant for South African small businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with BizBot SA via WhatsApp, we may collect:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number
  • Your business name and type
  • Messages you send to the bot (bookings, expenses, invoices, queries)
  • Customer records you create through the service
  • Transaction and payment data you log

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Operate the BizBot SA assistant and respond to your messages
  • Manage bookings, quotes, invoices, and expense records on your behalf
  • Send you automated reminders and weekly business reports
  • Improve the service over time
We do not sell your data to third parties. Your business data is yours.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in the European Union. We apply row-level security so your data is isolated from other businesses on the platform.

4. Third-Party Services

BizBot SA uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • WhatsApp Cloud API (Meta) — message delivery
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered message understanding
  • Supabase — secure database storage
  • Yoco — optional payment link generation (South Africa)
  • Render — cloud hosting

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.

5. Data Retention

We retain your business data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Phone numbers used for WhatsApp confirmation are encrypted at rest.

8. Children's Privacy

BizBot SA is intended for business owners and is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active users of material changes via WhatsApp message.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at:
